Sports Cars - Site Plan

Site Name

Sports Cars

This name represents a website dedicated to showcasing high-performance sports cars, focusing on their specifications and history.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a hub for sports car enthusiasts. It will feature performance specifications, historical context, and a gallery of high-quality images of various sports car models.

Scenarios

  • What are the top-performing sports cars of 2025?
  • Where can I find detailed specifications for my favorite sports car?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the site are:

  • Red (#D32F2F): Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Black (#333333): Used for text and navigation links.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for the background and text contrast.

Typography

The selected fonts for the site are:

  • Oswald: Used for headings and navigation links.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs.
